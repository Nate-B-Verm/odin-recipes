Lasagna

Description:

This recipe will show you how to make the most delicious sloppy joes you have ever tasted. You will find yourself coming back to this recipe on a regular basis.

This recipe is easy to make and uses minimal ingredients. Best of luck to you in your culinery endeavors!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Hamburger
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Turn on the burner to high
  2. Mix the hamburger, tomato sauce, and spices together in a pan
  3. Put the pan on the burner for 15-20 minutes mixing occasionally
  4. Serve the dish to your family
