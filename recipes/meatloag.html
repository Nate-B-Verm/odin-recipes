Lasagna

Description:

This recipe will show you how to make the most delicious meatloaf you have ever tasted. You will find yourself coming back to this recipe on a regular basis.

This recipe is easy to make and uses minimal ingredients. Best of luck to you in your culinery endeavors!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger
  • Ketchup
  • Barbecue Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Other Things

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. Mix the hamburger and other things together in a bowl
  3. Shape meat mixture into a loaf in a pan
  4. Mix the ketchup, brown sugar, and barbecue sauce in a bowl
  5. Apply the sauce mixture to the top of the meatloaf
  6. Put the dish into the oven for 45-60 minutes
  7. Serve the dish to your family
