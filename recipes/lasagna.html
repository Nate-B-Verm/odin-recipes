Lasagna

Description:

This recipe will show you how to make the most delicious lasagna you have ever tasted. You will find yourself coming back to this recipe on a regular basis.

This recipe is easy to make and uses minimal ingredients. Best of luck to you in your culinery endeavors!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Hamburger
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Swiss Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Farenheit
  2. Mix the hamburger, tomato sauce, parmesean cheese, and swiss Cheese together in a bowl
  3. Layer the lasagna noodles, spreading the mixture over each layer
  4. Put the dish into the oven for 30-45 minutes
  5. Serve the dish to your family
